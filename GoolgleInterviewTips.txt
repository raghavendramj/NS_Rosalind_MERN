Interview Process
 - 5 "onsite" virtual Hangouts interviews - 3 x DS/Algo, 1 system design and 1 Googleyness and Leadership

Interview Structure
   - Duration: 45mins for coding rounds and 60mins for system design round
   - Google Hangouts call (video is highly recommended)
   - Done on "Virtual Interviewing Platform"
   - Interviewer: Software Engineer from US
   - Interview dates: Weekdays
   - Timings: Evening: 7pm - 11pm IST

Action Step
   - Send me your preferred interview time slots
   - Be confident and enjoy the preparation process. ALL THE BEST!!

Concepts to Study for Coding rounds

Arrays, Stacks, Trees (Binary Trees, Binary Search Trees, Heap), Linked
List, Queue, Graphs, Union Find Data Structure, (DFS, BFS), Dynamic
Programming, Recursion & Backtracking, Divide & Conquer, Dictionaries
(Maps, Hash), Minimum spanning tree, String Algorithms: e.g. KMP.
